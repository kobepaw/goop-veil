## Why this matters
WiFi sensing research has shown that ordinary WiFi signals can reveal presence, movement, breathing, and heartbeat under some conditions. Public conversation is heating up fast, but practical defensive tooling is still early.

## What goop-veil does
### Detect
Scan for suspicious indicators such as Espressif hardware, hidden SSIDs, mesh behavior, and traffic patterns associated with sensing workflows.

### Mitigate
Use guarded router-based actions and recommendations to reduce sensing reliability where supported.

### Document
Generate structured documentation artifacts for internal review, incident handling, or counsel review.

## Trust / scope
- Software-only research preview
- Linux-first for scanning/capture workflows
- Heuristic detection, not proof or attribution
- Not a determination engine or compliance certification
- Firmware-related components currently experimental
