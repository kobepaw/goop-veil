## Comment-response library

### "Does this actually work?"
The honest answer is: parts are research-backed, but real-world performance varies by environment and attacker setup. goop-veil is meant to help detect, degrade, and document risk — not claim perfect prevention.

### "Is this legal / compliant?"
The project provides technical tooling and documentation artifacts, not legal determinations or compliance certification. Reporting and regulatory posture depends on jurisdiction and context.

### "Why not just use shielding?"
Physical mitigation matters and the tool includes room-assessment guidance, but many people need something they can test immediately with their existing router and no hardware purchase.

### "What routers work today?"
OpenWrt and UniFi are the clearest support paths. TP-Link is more model-dependent. ASUS Merlin is a good contributor target.

### "Why should I trust this?"
Because the repo is cautious about scope: it says research preview, documents limitations, avoids guaranteed claims, and ties mitigations back to published work.
