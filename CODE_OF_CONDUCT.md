# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the Contributor Covenant style of community expectations.

## Our standards
Examples of behavior that helps build a healthy project:
- being respectful and direct
- focusing on evidence and reproducibility
- correcting mistakes without dogpiling
- welcoming contributors with different experience levels
- keeping hype below truth

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- harassment or personal attacks
- publishing private information without consent
- intentionally misleading claims about project behavior
- hostile or abusive conduct toward contributors or reporters

## Scope
This applies in project spaces including issues, pull requests, discussions, and related public communication around the repository.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject content that violates these expectations.
